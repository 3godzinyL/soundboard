- Product/vendor: VB-CABLE by VB-Audio (Vincent Burel)
- Product page: https://vb-audio.com/Cable/
- Distribution terms: https://vb-audio.com/Services/licensing.htm
- Package release: October 2024
- Supported systems declared by the vendor: Windows XP through Windows 11,

- SHA-256: `B950E39F01AF1D04EA623C8F6D8EB9B6EA5C477C637295FABF20631C85116BFB`

VB-CABLE is donationware. The package is redistributed unmodified and its
vendor identity remains visible. Users must remain able to identify VB-Audio
as the driver vendor and donate or purchase a license if they find it useful.
Professional and volume deployments may require paid licensing under the
vendor's current terms.

The application never launches `VBCABLE_ControlPanel.exe`. On first production
start it extracts the package to a temporary directory, invokes the official
signed setup program, and removes the temporary files afterwards.
